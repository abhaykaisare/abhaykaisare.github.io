Why I’m Writing

Most people write to impress. That usually fails.

I’m writing for a different reason: to clarify my own thinking. If an idea can’t survive plain text, it probably isn’t a good idea.

This blog is intentionally simple. No algorithms, no feeds, no optimisation. Just words, published when they’re ready.

If something here is useful to you, that’s a bonus. If not, the act of writing was still worth it.

— Abhay
